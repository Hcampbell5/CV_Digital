Gels by G

Visit the website

"Gels by G" is a mobile nail technician service that offers gel nail treatments and products. The brand focuses on providing high-quality, long-lasting gel nails and skincare products that are cruelty-free and vegan-friendly.

This project showcases the development of our website, where users can explore our products, learn about ingredients, and purchase directly. We also provide insights into skincare tips and product usage.

What I learned

  • Developing a clean and modern skincare brand image online.
  • Creating an easy-to-navigate e-commerce website with user-friendly design.
  • Learning the importance of brand consistency and aesthetics in web design.
  • Implementing product descriptions, usage guides, and customer reviews effectively.
  • Understanding how to manage an online store from product inventory to order management.
